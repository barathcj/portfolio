TANGO - A Dance of the Web

Date Sep 2013 Worked with Javascript, HTML, CSS

Tango is a prototype for a news website created for HackCMU. It presents news in different layouts based on the distance the viewer is from the screen. It uses a web camera to estimate the distance of the user's face from the screen based on on which it has three different layouts (detail view, browse view and full screen video). Tango was awarded the 'Most Innovative Project' at HackCMU 2013 and was created in a single day.

MOBILE SPAM FILTER

Date June 2013 Worked with Android OS, Java, SQL

At Samsung, I was part of a team which developed a client side BAYESIAN SPAM FILTER It was designed to optimize storage space by utilizing time frame distribution and blacklisted domains to preset the attributes of Naïve Bayesian classification. This mechanism also aims at reducing the scope of tokenization of email, SMS messages based on the training data thereby reducing the power consumption of processing messages in the Android OS.
